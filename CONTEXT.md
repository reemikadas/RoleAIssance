# RoleAIssance

RoleAIssance helps a candidate turn verified career evidence into targeted job applications while retaining control over every claim.

## Language

**Verified Profile**:
The candidate-approved source of truth used for matching and application materials.
_Avoid_: AI profile, generated profile

**Master Resume**:
The candidate's original uploaded career document from which evidence may be extracted.
_Avoid_: Optimized resume, tailored resume

**Resume Analysis**:
A reviewable set of suggestions extracted from a Master Resume; it is not verified until the candidate approves it.
_Avoid_: Imported profile, automatic profile

**Career Section**:
One independently reviewable category of career evidence: Education, Work Experience, Projects, or Skills.
_Avoid_: Resume block, content bucket

**Tailored Resume**:
A job-specific document derived only from the Verified Profile and approved evidence.
_Avoid_: Master Resume, generated profile
